What Sets Us Apart

Special Features Included

Every course comes with extras that make your learning experience seamless and successful.

Pick-up & Drop-off

Your instructor collects you from your doorstep and drops you back after every lesson. No need to arrange transport to start your lesson.

Theory Test Support

Receive guidance and support for your theory test preparation. We help you tackle hazard perception and multiple-choice questions with confidence.

Mock Tests on Real Routes

Practise on the actual test routes used by your local test centre. Experience real test conditions so there are no surprises on the big day.

Multi-Language Instruction

Our instructors teach in English, Urdu, Hindi, Punjabi, and Pashto. Learn in the language you feel most comfortable with.

Specialist Training

Tailored instruction for nervous drivers, elderly learners, and young drivers. We adapt our teaching style to meet your individual needs and comfort level.

Residential Courses

Residential course options available for learners travelling from outside Greater Manchester. Combine accommodation with intensive training for a focused experience.

Manual

  • Drive both manual & automatic cars

Easier to Learn

Automatic

From £74/2hrs

No clutch, no gear changes. Automatic lessons let you focus entirely on the road, making learning quicker and less stressful for many students.

  • Easier and quicker to learn
  • Focus on the road, not gears

  • Slightly higher lesson cost
