Our Story

From One Instructor to Manchester's Best

A journey built on passion, dedication, and a commitment to excellence in driving education.

Impulse Driving School was founded in 2017 in Manchester with a clear mission: to make driving education accessible, effective, and enjoyable for everyone. What started as a single instructor with a passion for teaching has grown into one of Greater Manchester's most respected driving academies.

From those humble beginnings, we've grown to a team of 10+ ADI-approved professionals, each bringing their own expertise and teaching style to help students of all backgrounds and abilities succeed behind the wheel.

Today, we proudly cover all of Greater Manchester, having surpassed 20,000+ training hours and helped over 5,000 students earn their driving licence. Our consistent 93% first-time pass rate speaks to our unwavering commitment to quality instruction and student success.

We believe that learning to drive should be more than just passing a test — it's about building the skills, confidence, and road awareness that will keep you safe for a lifetime of driving.
